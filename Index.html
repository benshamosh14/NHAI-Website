About NHAI Consulting

NHAI Consulting is a student-led organization from Northern Highlands Regional High School that helps small local businesses adopt practical AI and automation solutions. Our team meets with businesses, identifies pain points, and develops AI-powered tools, workflows, and prototypes to improve operations, marketing, and customer experience.

Our Mission

To empower local businesses with practical, ethical, and affordable AI solutions while developing the next generation of innovators.
